# Field Worker Guide

## What You Do

Handle on-site logistics: scan incoming cases, verify inventory, and pick parts for installation work packages.

## Quick Access

- **Primary**: Supply List > Move-In - Scan and track case arrivals
- **Secondary**: Supply List > Inventory - Verify item counts
- **Also**: Supply List > Picking - Pick parts for work packages

## Key Tasks

- [ ] Scan case barcodes as they arrive on site
- [ ] Update case locations after moving
- [ ] Verify inventory counts match expected quantities
- [ ] Pick parts lists for installation crews
- [ ] Report discrepancies immediately

## Tips

- Use your mobile device for all scanning operations
- Check the Field Dashboard for your daily assignments
- Sync your device before starting work in low-connectivity areas
- Report damage or shortages before unpacking
